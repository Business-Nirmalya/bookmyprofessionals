Refund & Cancellation Policy

Understand our refund process, cancellation terms, and how we handle disputes for services booked through BookMyProfessionals.com.

Last Updated: March 31, 2024 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Policy Overview

At BookMyProfessionals.com, we strive to ensure a seamless experience for both customers and professionals. This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which cancellations and refunds are processed for services booked through our platform.

Important: Since BookMyProfessionals.com acts as an intermediary platform connecting customers with independent professionals, refund policies may vary by service category and professional. We are committed to facilitating fair resolutions for all parties involved.

Cancellation by Customer

As a customer, you have the right to cancel a booking based on the following guidelines:

Standard Cancellation Terms

Cancellation TimeframeRefund EligibilityNotes
24+ hours before serviceFull Refund100% of the booking amount refunded
12-24 hours before service50% Refund50% of the booking amount refunded
Less than 12 hours before serviceNo RefundCancellation fee applies
No-show / Last minuteNo RefundService considered delivered

Note: Some service categories may have different cancellation policies. Always confirm cancellation terms with the professional at the time of booking. Makeup artists, event planners, and wedding services often have stricter cancellation policies.

Cancellation by Professional

If a professional cancels a confirmed booking, we will take the following steps:

  • Full Refund: You will receive a 100% refund of any amount paid
  • Alternative Professional: We will attempt to arrange an alternative professional at no additional cost
  • Priority Rebooking: You will get priority assistance for rescheduling
  • No Cancellation Fees: You will not incur any fees for cancellations initiated by professionals

Good to know: If a professional cancels last minute, we will expedite finding a replacement and may offer a discount on your next booking as a goodwill gesture.

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Service Not Completed: If the professional is unable to complete the full service as agreed
  • Quality Issues: If the service quality is significantly below the agreed standard
  • Scope Reduction: If you reduce the scope of service after booking but before service delivery
  • Professional Delay: If the professional arrives significantly late and cannot complete the full service

The amount of partial refund will be determined based on the portion of service not delivered or the impact of quality issues.

Service-Specific Policies

Different service categories may have specific refund terms. Please review the policies for your service category:

Makeup & Beauty Services

  • Cancellation within 48 hours for bridal makeup: 50% refund
  • Cancellation within 24 hours: No refund
  • Deposits for bridal packages are non-refundable

Event & Wedding Services

  • Cancellation 30+ days before event: 90% refund
  • Cancellation 15-29 days before event: 50% refund
  • Cancellation less than 15 days: No refund
  • Deposits and advance payments are non-refundable

Home Services (Electricians, Plumbers, Carpenters, Painters, Cleaning)

  • Cancellation 24+ hours before: Full refund
  • Cancellation 12-24 hours: 50% refund
  • Cancellation less than 12 hours: No refund

Solar Panel Installation

  • Site survey fee: Non-refundable after survey completion
  • Cancellation after equipment order: Equipment costs deducted
  • Cancellation after installation begins: No refund for work completed

Tutoring Services

  • 24+ hours notice for session cancellation: Full refund or reschedule
  • Less than 24 hours notice: Session considered delivered
  • Package cancellations: Pro-rated refund for unused sessions

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Reach out via WhatsApp at +91 70087 43587 or email at bookings@bookmyprofessionals.com
  2. Provide Details: Share your booking details, reason for cancellation, and any supporting evidence (photos, messages, etc.)
  3. Review: Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours
  4. Resolution: We will coordinate with the professional to process the refund
  5. Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval

Refund Timeline: Once approved, refunds are processed via the original payment method. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take 3-10 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

Non-Refundable Items

The following are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Deposits and Advance Payments: Non-refundable deposits for wedding and event services
  • Site Survey Fees: Fees for site visits and assessments
  • Equipment Costs: Materials purchased specifically for your project (solar panels, custom materials, etc.)
  • Travel Fees: Professional travel charges (if incurred)
  • Services Already Rendered: Any portion of service already completed
  • No-Show Charges: If you are not available at the scheduled time

Dispute Resolution

If you and the professional cannot agree on a refund, we will help mediate the dispute:

  1. Documentation: Provide all relevant documentation (messages, photos, invoices, etc.)
  2. Review: Our team will review the evidence and communicate with both parties
  3. Mediation: We will propose a fair resolution based on the facts
  4. Final Decision: If no agreement is reached, we may make a final determination based on our platform policies
  5. Alternative: If the dispute cannot be resolved, you may pursue legal remedies as per our Terms of Service

Important: We encourage all customers and professionals to communicate clearly and resolve disputes amicably. Our platform is built on trust, and we're here to help facilitate fair outcomes.

Exceptions and Special Cases

Refunds may be considered in exceptional circumstances:

  • Medical Emergencies: With valid medical documentation, we may offer flexible cancellation terms
  • Natural Disasters: In case of unforeseen natural events, full refunds may be provided
  • Professional Misconduct: Full refunds for verified cases of professional misconduct
  • Safety Concerns: If there are valid safety concerns, immediate refunds will be processed

All exceptions require supporting documentation and will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
