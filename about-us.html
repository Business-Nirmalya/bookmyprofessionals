Our Story

How BookMyProfessionals became Odisha's trusted service platform

From a Simple Idea to Odisha's Trusted Platform

BookMyProfessionals was born from a simple observation - finding reliable professionals in Odisha was challenging. Whether it was a makeup artist for a wedding, an electrician for a home repair, or a tutor for a student, people struggled to find trusted professionals they could count on.

Starting from Bhubaneswar in 2024, we set out to change this. We began by carefully vetting professionals, verifying their credentials, and building a platform where customers could find them easily. Word spread quickly, and soon we expanded to Cuttack, Puri, and then across Odisha.

Today, we proudly serve over 30 cities across Odisha, with a network of 1000+ verified professionals. Our WhatsApp-based booking system has made it incredibly easy for customers to get the services they need, whenever they need them.
