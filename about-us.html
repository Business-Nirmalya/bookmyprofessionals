About Us

Odisha's Most Trusted Platform for Booking Verified Professionals

Our Story

BookMyProfessionals was founded in 2024 with a simple yet powerful vision - to make professional services accessible, reliable, and hassle-free for everyone across Odisha. What started as a small initiative in Bhubaneswar has quickly grown into Odisha's most trusted platform for booking verified professionals.

We recognized that finding trustworthy professionals for home services, events, and personal needs was a challenge. People often struggled with unreliable service providers, hidden costs, and uncertainty about quality. That's why we created a platform that connects customers with thoroughly verified professionals who are committed to excellence.

Today, we proudly serve over 30 cities across Odisha, with a growing network of 1000+ verified professionals who have completed 10,000+ successful bookings. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to platform for professional services in Odisha.

Our Mission

To empower every individual and business in Odisha with instant access to verified, reliable professionals who deliver exceptional service quality, transparency, and value for money.

Our Vision

To become Odisha's most preferred platform for professional services, creating a ecosystem where trust, quality, and convenience come together to transform how people access services.
